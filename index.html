Help & Connection Guide

What does optimized launch do?

Optimized launch opens your selected website through a YouTube redirect URL to request Tesla's fullscreen media context. This is a practical workaround, not an official Tesla feature, and it may change with future Tesla software updates.

Why do I need to sign in again?

Tesla may use different browser contexts for normal browsing and fullscreen media. If a service asks you to sign in again, complete the login directly on that website after launching it.

Why not embed websites here?

Streaming services, DRM, cookies, and login flows work best as top-level pages. This launcher does not proxy, iframe, or intercept third-party websites.

Which mode should I use?

Use Quick Apps for the fastest Tesla-first flow. Use the URL field only when you want to launch a custom website.
