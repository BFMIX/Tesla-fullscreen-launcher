Help & Connection Guide

Why aren't my logins shared?

Tesla isolates standard browsing and Fullscreen Media mode in separate Chromium profiles. You must log in directly while in Fullscreen mode.

Login button does nothing?

Some sites use popup windows for authorization which are sandboxed. Use "Assisted Login" (long-press a card) to load the login page directly in full screen.

Can't login to Google / YouTube?

Google blocks embedded browsers. Workaround: Open Plex or Spotify in Fullscreen, click Sign in with Google, and log in. Google cookies are shared, so you will be signed in on YouTube automatically!
